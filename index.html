Fortnite

Fortnite: Battle Royale is an online survival game made by Epic games. 100 players land on a large island looking for weapons, materials and healable items. The last player standing gets a Victory royale. At its peak, Fortnite averaged around 75 million daily players. Nowadays, it has cooled down to a moderate 6 million daily players. Fortnite has single-handedly boosted Epic games to a multi-billion dollar company. A large part of their revenue coming from ingame items like skins, dances, and their seasonal battle pass. In 2020 alone, Fortnite earned over 5.1 billion$ in cosmetic items alone.
